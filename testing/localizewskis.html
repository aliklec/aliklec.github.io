ℹ About Us

        
The people behind Localizewskis

        

          

            Localizewskis was founded by two sisters with a combined 20 years of experience in localization and content design. We've worked across industries, languages, and platforms, from enterprise software to creative marketing campaigns.
          

          

            We built this site to share the tools, resources, and knowledge we wish we had when we were starting out. Every tool in the Lingo Suite runs in your browser for free, no accounts or uploads required.
          

          

            Whether you're a freelance translator, a localization manager at a tech company, or just getting into the field, we're here to help. Have a question or want to work together? Get in touch.
